# GLCDI Data Space: Participant Policy Inputs Summary

This document summarizes the data policy inputs provided by participants during the GLCDI Data Space prototype phase workshop (January–September 2026).

## 1. Participant: Elijah Goodwin

- **Organization:** Stone Barns Center
- **Dataset Name:** SBC Grazing Soil Health Measurements 2020-2025
- **Brief Description:** Soil sampled annually in late July/early August to 30cm using the spade method from 16 rotationally grazed pastures.
- **Access Policies:** Visible to any verified GLCDI member, researchers, and producers.
- **Permitted Purposes (Agreement-Backed):** Regional benchmarking, Agronomic model training and calibration, Scientific research and publication, Conservation planning.
- **Prohibited Uses (Agreement-Backed):** Redistribution to third parties, Commercial use, Use by buyers, food companies, or supply chain actors.
- **Time Limit (System-Enforced):** Ongoing — no fixed end date.
- **Payment (System-Enforced):** No payment required.
- **Attribution (Agreement-Backed):** Anyone using this data must credit Elijah Goodwin and GLCDI in any publication or report.
- **Anonymization (Agreement-Backed):** Comfortable with farm-level data being used without anonymization.
- **Share-back of Insights (Agreement-Backed):** Anyone who derives insights must share those results back.
- **Additional Suggestions:** Software should handle multiple uses for the same dataset (commercial vs. academic), standardized clickable tags (e.g., soil sampling depths), and clarity on grazing management detail levels.

## 2. Participant: Byron Palmer

- **Organization:** Sonoma Mountain Institute
- **Dataset Name:** Copy of Pasture Map Export
- **Brief Description:** 2,480 records documenting grazing activities from Dec 2016 through April 2026. Metrics for livestock management, including Date Time In and Date Time Out.
- **Access Policies:** Visible to any verified GLCDI member, researchers, producers, public, corporates, and supply chain actors.
- **Payment (System-Enforced):** $500 USD per dataset.
- **Share-back of Insights (Agreement-Backed):** Requires tables, analysis, reports, and original data files to be shared back.
- **Re-consultation:** Requests to be explicitly re-consulted if a corporate participant or aggregator joins the group.

## 3. Participant: Chang Zhao

- **Organization:** University of Florida
- **Dataset Name:** State of Florida Grazing land SOC measurements 2022-2024
- **Brief Description:** Soil organic carbon measurements across 22 dairy and 14 beef cattle farms. Samples collected from 0-15cm, 15-30cm, and 30-90cm layers using random transects.
- **Access Policies:** Visible to verified GLCDI members, researchers, producers, public, corporates, and supply chain actors.
- **Attribution (Agreement-Backed):** Anyone using this data must credit me and GLCDI in any publication or report.
- **Additional Conditions:** For academic or scientific research, researchers must contact data owners directly before using data. Scholarly publications require prior discussion regarding use, publication plans, collaboration, acknowledgment, or co-authorship.

## 4. Participant: Laura Kaminsky

- **Organization:** Pasa Sustainable Agriculture
- **Dataset Name:** Pasa Soil Health Benchmark Study Data 2016-2024
- **Brief Description:** Data from 250+ pasture or hay fields in the Mid-Atlantic and Northeast US. Includes soil health indicators (LOI, SOC) and quantitative soil management indicators (days of living cover, grazing events, stocking density, inputs).
- **Access Policies:** Visible to verified GLCDI members, researchers, producers, public, corporates, and supply chain actors.
- **Additional Conditions:** Results must not be published without her review.
- **Uncertainties:** Unsure about payment for the dataset (whether to charge at all, how much, and if only specific actors should pay).

## 5. Participant: Aarushi Jhatro

- **Organization:** White Buffalo Land Trust
- **Dataset Name:** Jalama Canyon Ranch Rangeland SOC measurements 2021-2025
- **Brief Description:** Soil Organic Carbon, Dry Combustion measured at ~30 locations across 420 acres in California. Depths 0-15cm, 15-30cm. Years: 2022, 2023, 2025.
- **Anonymization (Agreement-Backed):** Use case dependent.
- **Additional Conditions:** Results must not be published without her review.
- **Suggestions:** Anonymization, attribution, and payment could be use-case dependent options to be defined after a conversation with the Data Accessor. Suggested an option to add supplemental information via attachments.
- **Re-consultation:** Wants to be re-consulted under all circumstances (new cohort, corporate participant onboarded, outside region requests access).
